# 中华神兽水墨介绍视频 · 剧本分析报告

## 整体风格建议
- **视觉风格**:中国传统水墨画风(Chinese ink wash painting / shuimo)
- **核心语言**:飞白、晕染、留白、墨色浓淡五色(焦/浓/重/淡/清)
- **画面基调**:写意不写实,大块留白,虚实相生,神兽以水墨笔触呈现
- **节奏**:舒缓、庄严、有呼吸感(每个分镜 6 秒)
- **色调**:以水墨黑白灰为主,神兽本色作点缀(青龙偏青、白虎偏赭、朱雀偏朱、玄武偏玄黑、麒麟偏金)

## 人物清单(即 5 神兽)

### 1. 青龙 (Azure Dragon · 东方木)
- **身份**:四象之首,掌东方,主木,司春
- **外貌描述**:龙首鹿角、蛇身鱼鳞、鹰爪虎掌,通体青黑色水墨渲染,龙鬃飞扬,双目如电
- **形态风格**:水墨写意,龙身蜿蜒于云海之间,墨色浓淡变化体现立体感
- **性格特点**:威严、生机、刚健
- **AI 生图提示词**:
```
A majestic Chinese Azure Dragon, ink wash painting style, dragon head with deer antlers, long serpentine body covered in fish scales, sharp eagle claws, flowing mane and whiskers, body coiling through rolling clouds, deep ink black with subtle cyan-green tints, dynamic flying pose, traditional Chinese shuimo technique with flying white brushstrokes, vast white space around the creature, calligraphy influence, masterpiece, high contrast
```

### 2. 白虎 (White Tiger · 西方金)
- **身份**:四象之一,掌西方,主金,司秋
- **外貌描述**:白额虎纹,通体以赭石与淡墨晕染,虎目圆睁,利爪踏山石
- **形态风格**:写意虎形,身姿矫健,蹲踞于西岭山巅,墨色有骨
- **性格特点**:勇猛、肃杀、威武
- **AI 生图提示词**:
```
A mighty Chinese White Tiger, ink wash painting style, broad forehead with the character 王, muscular body in pale ochre and diluted ink, fierce round eyes, sharp fangs, powerful claws gripping mountain rocks, long striped tail sweeping, sitting in a powerful crouching pose on a western mountain peak, traditional shuimo brushwork with bold strokes and splashed ink effects, vast misty void background, masterpiece, cinematic
```

### 3. 朱雀 (Vermilion Bird · 南方火)
- **身份**:四象之一,掌南方,主火,司夏
- **外貌描述**:凤冠鸡首、鹤颈鸳腮、鸿前麟后、燕颔鸡喙,羽色以朱砂与淡墨相融
- **形态风格**:展翅翔于南天,长尾飘举,墨羽带赤,火光隐隐
- **性格特点**:炽烈、光明、华美
- **AI 生图提示词**:
```
A glorious Chinese Vermilion Bird (Zhuque), ink wash painting style, phoenix-like bird with rooster head and phoenix crown, long graceful neck, flowing tail feathers stretching behind, wings spread wide mid-flight, plumage rendered in vermilion red and crimson ink bleeding into black, surrounded by swirling flame-like clouds in the south sky, traditional Chinese shuimo technique, bold calligraphic brushwork, dynamic soaring pose, vast white space, masterpiece
```

### 4. 玄武 (Black Tortoise · 北方水)
- **身份**:四象之一,掌北方,主水,司冬
- **外貌描述**:龟蛇同体,龟壳厚重以焦墨皴擦,蛇身盘绕于龟背之上
- **形态风格**:沉静盘踞于北海玄冰之上,墨色深沉,偶有水波飞白
- **性格特点**:沉稳、深邃、玄秘
- **AI 生图提示词**:
```
A solemn Chinese Black Tortoise (Xuanwu), ink wash painting style, giant tortoise shell with deep burnt-ink texture and crackled patterns, a black serpent coiled around the shell with its head raised and forked tongue flickering, the creature resting on dark northern ocean waves, misty void around, heavy ink layering technique with subtle white splashes for water foam, traditional shuimo style, low angle heroic view, masterpiece, dramatic
```

### 5. 麒麟 (Qilin · 中央土)
- **身份**:瑞兽之首,掌中央,主土,祥瑞之征
- **外貌描述**:鹿身龙尾、马蹄牛尾、龙头带角、全身披鳞甲
- **形态风格**:踏祥云而来,瑞气千条,墨色温润如玉
- **性格特点**:仁和、祥瑞、至尊
- **AI 生图提示词**:
```
A celestial Chinese Qilin (auspicious beast), ink wash painting style, deer-like body with dragon scales covering the torso, ox tail and horse hooves, dragon head with curved antlers, single horn on forehead, gentle wise eyes, walking gracefully through auspicious swirling clouds in the central sky, body rendered in warm ink with subtle golden-ochre tints, traditional shuimo brushwork with elegant flowing lines, soft white negative space, masterpiece, divine atmosphere
```

## 场景清单

### 1. 东方云海 (East Sea of Clouds)
- **场景类型**:室外 / 天界
- **环境描述**:东方天际,层层云海翻涌如波,朝阳初升,金光从云缝透出
- **关键元素**:云海、远山剪影、霞光
- **色调氛围**:青墨色 + 暖金,生机勃发
- **AI 生图提示词**:
```
A vast eastern sea of clouds at dawn, Chinese ink wash painting style, layered rolling clouds in deep ink and pale grey, distant mountain silhouettes as faint brushstrokes on the horizon, golden sun rays piercing through cloud gaps, vast empty white space in upper sky, traditional shuimo panoramic view, atmospheric perspective, masterpiece
```

- **色调氛围**:赭墨色 + 飞白,肃杀苍茫
- **AI 生图提示词**:
```
A towering western mountain ridge, Chinese ink wash painting style, jagged peaks with dark burnt-ink texture, pine trees as silhouette brushstrokes clinging to cliffs, swirling white mist around the mid-slopes, vast void sky above, traditional shuimo technique with flying white dry brushwork, dramatic and solemn atmosphere, masterpiece
```

### 3. 南方火海 (Southern Flaming Sky)
